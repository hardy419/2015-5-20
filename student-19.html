Theme of the portfolio

 

Dream

 

Artist’s statement

 

Dreams are like wishes, I made dreams figurative by making them as the coins in the wishing well and shooting stars. The main idea is the value of dream. Coins in the wishing well represent a kind of effort people given for their dreams, as people believed that gods are unlikely to give away anything for nothing, people pay few cents for their dreams. On the contrary, stars represent the beauty and purity of dreams people have, especially when they are young. People at birth are the pure water in the well while money contaminates their dreams day by day. Stars and coins shines, but only stars shines perpetually and even lead others in the dark.

It is unworthy to evaluate dreams money; when time past, they no longer shine. “Connect the dots puzzle”, the game we often play in childhood. The idea is like we pursue our dream step by step. It is attractive for us that we do not know what we are going to be in the future. The numbers represent age. It tells that money dominant our life, while reality makes us forget about the dreams.

 

Inspired by the concept of “gain and lose”, we must sacrifice/get rid of something in order to gain something. We ought to relieve some burden in order to fly higher. Alive is about living physically and mentally. We must be enriched, like a doll filled with cotton.

Hugging pillows give us a sense of security, this inspires me to use cotton in my work. Cotton, the material that protect our wounds; though chasing after dreams could be difficult and risky, we would never afraid to fall as long as we hold onto our dreams and believe in ourselves. Clouds, full of imaginations and fantasies, which are symbols of dreams.  We sometimes think that they are really far away from us, but they are actually as close as water in our body. It is about the change of forms, if we have passion to heat up the water, water could change into vapor and clouds eventually.
