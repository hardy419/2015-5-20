Theme of the portfolio

 

魚情未了

 

創作者自白

Artist’s Statement

 

魚隨伴着我成長, 是我童年為一的朋友。作品首先表達了我對魚的情感, 然後從個人的層面走到不同層面如人類、動物、社會等等,並從不同的角度分析現實世界。 作品主要借魚的世界來諷刺現實生活的殘酷以及人類醜陋的行為。最後也借此作品表達我對和諧社會渴望。

I have a pet fish when I was a child, it is my close friend. I have much precious memories with my pet fish. The artworks express my personal feeling to the fish. The earlier work enclosed more personal experiences. The approaches of later works are more social issues and environmental matters, in order to explore the reality. The artworks through the fish images reflect the cruelty and selfish behaviour of human. Finally, I want to express my wishes of harmony and peace.
