梁曉心

Leung Hiu Sum

 

香港浸會大學附屬學校王錦輝中小學

Hong Kong Baptist University Affiliated School Wong Kam Fai Secondary and Primary School

 

 

Theme of the portfolio

 

It’s All About Myself

 

Artist’s statement

 

My life is always surrounded by dreams, memories, and social things. According to what I have experienced though out my secondary school life, I did artworks to present my feelings and ideas in different period of time.

 

Life is just like a puzzle, different parts including my interest and dreams, etc, combined together to form who I am nowadays. I started my portfolio from this “puzzle”, and used the format of collage to show others what kind of people am I. Next, I continued my portfolio by an acrylic painting about my dreams. Dream is an important part of my life, having a dream and the courage to fight of it makes me never lost from the path of my life and makes me become so special than others. Using balloons and buildings, I tried to express my desire towards being the top in different curriculum. Follow by, I went back to memories. Sometimes, after you walked a very long path to fulfil your dreams, you may feel tired and would like to have some help from others. This made me think of my family and parents. My portfolio comes to the third project and I just paper an LED light to crate “Silhouette of Memories”. Silhouette give us a large area to imagine, by using paper and LED light, I would also like others to imagine what they experience in the past with their parents and families. Last, time flies, in the last secondary school year, I started aware more about our society and politics. Then, I created the project “Looking through the Frame”.

 

All projects in my portfolio record my growth. From thinking about my own to my family; from family to our society…… all these show the change of my thought and show that I am growing up. Visual art portfolio in my secondary school life finally end as a diary, which let others know more about me, as well as let me understand more myself.
