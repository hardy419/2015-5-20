背景

Background

配合「高中學生視覺藝術作品集展覽2014/15」，藝術教育組舉辦兩場學生分享會，並邀請參展的學生 及其指導教師就視覺藝術科作品集的建立作分享。

To complement the exhibition, “Exhibition of Senior Secondary School Students’ Visual Arts Portfolios”, two sessions of students’ sharing session are held, and participating students of the exhibition and their teachers are invited to share on portfolio building in Visual Arts.
