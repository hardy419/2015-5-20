序言

「高中學生視覺藝術作品集展覽」由教育局 藝術教育組舉辦，目的是透過展示高中學生 的視覺藝術作品集，以支援高中視覺藝術科 課程及評估的推行，並為學生及教師提供互 動學習的機會。今年，我們首次邀請全港中 學參與，參加者均為應考2014年香港中學文 憑試視覺藝術科的學生。

 

是次展出二十個涵蓋不同形式藝術創作的作 品集。評審團按作品集是否能展示學生真實 的探究過程，以及香港中學文憑試視覺藝術 科的評核準則等範疇，選出獲獎及展出的作 品集。本年度評審團由視覺藝術教育專業人 士組成，包括楊懷俸先生、劉仲嚴博士、梁 海勇先生、陳美珍女士，以及藝術教育組代 表。

 

展覽包括研究工作簿複製本和作品集中部分 完成作品。場內亦播放由參展學生介紹其作 品集的錄影片段。我們衷心多謝各位評判， 以及所有參加的學校、教師和學生的熱心支 持。我們期望能為提升高中視覺藝術科課程 及評估，繼續共勉。

Preface

The “Exhibition of Senior Secondary School Students’ Visual Arts Portfolios” is organised by the Arts Education Section of the Education Bureau. It aims to support the implementation of the senior secondary (SS) Visual Arts curriculum and assessment through displaying SS students’ visual arts portfolios; as well as to promote student-teacher interactive learning. This is the first time that all secondary schools were invited for participation. The participants of the exhibition were students who sat for Visual Arts in the 2014 Hong Kong Diploma of Secondary Education Examination.

 

20 portfolios with a variety of art forms, illustrating the authentic progress of work basing on the assessment criteria of the Visual Arts School-based Assessment of HKDSE were selected by a judging panel for award and display. The panel was composed of visual arts education professionals, including Mr. YEUNG Wai-fung, Dr. LAU Chung-yim, Mr. LEUNG Hoi-yung, Ms CHAN Mei-chuen, and representatives of the Arts Education Section.

 

On display are the reproductions of the participants’ research workbook and selected art pieces from the portfolios. Video clippings of students introducing their works are also on show for sharing. Our gratitude is expressed to the judges, as well as the participating schools, teachers and students for their enthusiastic participation. We hope that we will continue to work together for enhancing the learning and teaching effectiveness of the SS Visual Arts curriculum and assessment.
